Extended Support from Veeva

Objective:

  • BREEZE Team would work with EAM\Escalation Manager from Veeva to resolve the issues.
  • Veeva support team will be available during critical submission 24*7 to support Publishing issues.

Benefit:

  • Increased quality level of Vendor support by appointing dedicated SPOC with overall business context awareness.
  • Reduced risk for delay in addressing Veeva related issues.

Submission Key User Prioritization

Objectives

  • Hypercare service will be enabled for the submission users, so that along with BREEZE all other infrastructure related issues like Teams , DoD, network etc will be addressed on priority.
  • All tickets related to submission will be considered as priority and will be routed through GSD to support team during weekends.
  • Submission tickets will be flagged and support team to consider this flag and prioritize ticket resolution.

Monitoring

Objectives

  • Secure application operations for all submission relevant application from system point of view as well as from end-user point of view.

Benefit

  • Early notification on issues/outages to responsible teams to react quickly
  • Detailed information on system health status with additional history data and KPI trends
  • Completely automated system observations without required manual interactions by 24x7(Service Tracer)
